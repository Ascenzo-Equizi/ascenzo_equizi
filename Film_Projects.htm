B.O.B The Robot

I made B.O.B The robot in my first year at AIE (Academy Of Interactive Entertainment)
In this course I got the oppertunity to learn how to model and animate with the application Maya from Autodesk
I also got to learn how to use the application Davinci Resolve which is where I edited this short film
I hope you enjoy B.O.B The Robots little adventure in a toy store

Aplications Used:
Autodesk - Maya
Davinci Resolve
Krita

Summery Of Short Film:
B.O.B is toy robot that has the ability to learn and grow, B.O.B Stands for Bond Of Brothers because the B.O.B robots Stick together.
B.O.B notices that his power supply is on low power and he needs to find a new power source before he loses all power and forgets everything he has learned
While B.O.B is fightning for his life he comes across many challenges but is forced to keep fightning.
