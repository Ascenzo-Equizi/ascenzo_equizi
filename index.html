Skill Set

I offer a wide range of creative services across video production, 3D design, and event work, tailored to meet the needs of diverse clients. My expertise includes color grading, motion graphics, and visual effects supervision, ensuring seamless integration of visuals and effects during post-production. I excel in 3D texturing, shading, and environment design, crafting immersive worlds and realistic assets, while also specializing in virtual production using Unreal Engine. On set, I provide professional camera operation, creative lighting design, and production planning to bring projects to life. Additionally, I offer wedding and event videography, as well as DJ and MC services, combining technical skill with an engaging presence. For those looking to refine their craft, I provide training in industry-standard software and consulting on efficient production workflows. Whether you need a polished showreel, expertly edited photos, or dynamic storytelling, my goal is to deliver high-quality, impactful results tailored to your vision.

Video Production & Post-Production

I specialize in delivering high-quality video production and post-production services that bring stories to life. From precise color grading to enhance mood and tone to dynamic motion graphics for titles and animations, I ensure that every frame serves the narrative. My expertise also extends to visual effects supervision, collaborating closely with directors and on-set teams to plan and execute seamless VFX integration. In post-production, I focus on crafting engaging content through expert sound editing and design, blending visuals and audio into cohesive, impactful productions. Whether it’s refining dialogue, syncing sound effects, or creating immersive audio experiences, I bring a polished touch to every project.

3D and VFX Services

With a strong foundation in 3D and visual effects, I offer services ranging from texturing and shading to designing entire 3D environments. My work involves creating highly detailed, realistic assets that bring virtual worlds to life. Using tools like Autodesk Maya and Adobe Substance Painter, I ensure precision and artistry in every 3D element. I also specialize in dynamic effects and simulations, crafting realistic effects like fire, water, explosions, and more. In addition, I utilize Unreal Engine for virtual production, enabling real-time filmmaking and pre-visualization to push creative boundaries in storytelling.

Cinematography & On-Set Services

On set, I provide professional cinematography services, including camera operation, lighting design, and production planning. With a keen eye for composition and a passion for visual storytelling, I collaborate closely with directors and crews to achieve the perfect shot. My experience spans diverse projects, from short films to documentaries, ensuring high-quality results every time. I also excel in designing creative lighting setups that enhance mood and support the narrative. Whether working with natural light or constructing elaborate setups, I bring technical expertise and artistic intuition to every production.

Photography

Through the lens, I transform ordinary moments into extraordinary visuals, capturing the essence of every subject with precision and creativity. My photography style blends technical expertise with an artistic eye, delivering images that are not just pictures but stories frozen in time. From cinematic portraits to dynamic event photography, I focus on creating compositions that evoke emotion and leave a lasting impression. Whether it’s the subtle play of light and shadow, the vivid energy of a candid moment, or the fine details of a carefully crafted scene, my work celebrates the beauty in every frame. Each project is approached with a tailored vision, ensuring that every shot reflects the unique personality and narrative of my clients. With a commitment to quality and an eye for the extraordinary, I aim to turn your vision into stunning visuals that resonate long after the moment has passed.
